Pediatric Department

The team includes skilled pediatricians, nurses, and specialists dedicated to promoting the health and well-being of young patients.

Medical Department

The medical department is committed to delivering comprehensive healthcare services to address a broad spectrum of medical needs. Surgical

General surgery department 

General surgery department

The General surgery department at KCMC offer a range of super-specialized services that play a crucial role in diagnosing and treating a variety of medical conditions. These services encompass a broad spectrum of surgical subspecialties, ensuring comprehensive care for patients. One key area of specialization within general surgery is minimally invasive surgery, which includes laparoscopic and robotic procedures. These techniques use small incisions and advanced technology to perform surgeries with less trauma, shorter recovery times, and reduced scarring. Gastrointestinal surgeons focus on issues related to the digestive system, such as hernias, gallbladder problems, and bowel disorders, while vascular surgeons handle conditions affecting blood vessels, such as aneurysms or peripheral artery disease. Another super-specialized field within general surgery is surgical oncology, which involves the removal of tumors and cancerous tissues. Trauma surgery addresses emergency cases, particularly those involving severe injuries from accidents or violence. Furthermore, transplant surgery deals with organ transplantation, and pediatric surgeons specialize in surgeries for children. The general surgery department collaborates with numerous other specialties, including radiology, anesthesiology, and critical care, to provide comprehensive patient care. These super-specialized services ensure that patients receive the most effective, evidence-based treatments, tailored to their unique medical conditions, ultimately improving outcomes and enhancing the quality of healthcare.

Eye

Eye Department 

Eye Department

The ophthalmology department offers highly specialized services focused on diagnosing and treating eye conditions. This includes advanced procedures like laser eye surgery, cataract extraction, glaucoma management, and retinal surgeries. Additionally, they provide precise vision assessments, contact lens fittings, and tailored treatment plans to ensure optimal eye health and visual acuity.

Demartology

Demartology Department 

Demartology Department

The Dermatology department provides a wide range of specialized services dedicated to the diagnosis and treatment of skin-related conditions. This encompasses advanced procedures such as dermatologic surgery, laser skin treatments, and mole removal. Furthermore, our team offers comprehensive skin assessments, skin cancer screenings, and customized treatment regimens to promote healthy skin and enhance your overall dermatological well-being.

Orthopaedics

Orthopaedics Department 

orthopaedics department

The orthopaedics department provides highly specialised services with an emphasis on musculoskeletal disorders diagnosis and treatment. These include delicate spine procedures, complex joint replacements, sports injury management, and cutting-edge fracture care. Their proficiency in the treatment of bones, joints, and muscles guarantees that patients receive individualised care for a faster recovery and higher quality of life.

gynaecology

gynaecology Department 

Obstetrics and gynaecology Department

The Obstetrics and gynaecology department offers highly specialised care for women's reproductive health. They provide comprehensive services including prenatal care, childbirth assistance, fertility treatments, gynaecological surgeries, and management of complex conditions like endometriosis and high-risk pregnancies. Their expertise ensures women receive tailored, top-notch care throughout their reproductive journey.
